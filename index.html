FROGGER

Frogger is a classic retro arcade game that has sold millions of copies since it's release in 1981. Although it has a basic concept of directing frogs home by crossing a busy road and river, it has garnered a lot of attention and positive reviews. It was followed by several clones and sequels and is now engraved in video game history.
